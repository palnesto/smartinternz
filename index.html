VIRTUAL ADMIN INTERN JOB DESCRIPTION

Internship Duration: 2 - 3 Months

Your virtual Internship journey is designed to prepare you for the next big step of your successful career in salesforce ecosystem.

Live Hands-on Training

18 Hours (6 Days@ 3 Hrs per day)

Self paced E-Learning Courses (42 Hours on Salesforce Trailblazer)

You will get 20 Days timeframe to complete Admin Course modules

Complete Admin Super Badge (40 Hours on Salesforce Trailblazer)

You will get another 20 Days timeframe to complete admin super set along with project development

Ask a Mentor Sessions

We organise 4 Mentor sessions during super badges completion phase to address any roadblocks you come across

Soft skills sessions

We organise a special soft skills development session to prepare you for the industry

Career Fair/Corporate connect

Successfully completed Students from final years of their graduation will get an opportunity to attend career fair with clientele of salesforce ecosystem
